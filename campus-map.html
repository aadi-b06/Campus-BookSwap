Safety Tips for Book Exchanges

Meet in Public Places

Always meet in well-lit, public areas with plenty of people around.

Bring a Friend

If possible, bring a friend along, especially for first-time meetings.

Share Your Plans

Let someone know where you're going and when you expect to return.

Use Secure Payments

Use our secure payment system instead of carrying cash.
